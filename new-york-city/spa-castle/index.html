Spa Castle

Type: Korean Spa · Neighborhood: College Point, Queens

Price: from $85

DIP Score: 7.5

Hours / Access: Daily 9am–10pm · $110 weekends

Five floors of Korean spa in College Point with a gold pyramid sauna and no time limit. Not a cool place. A very good place — the kind you drive 45 minutes to and wonder why you don't go every week. Recently renovated, still completely no-frills.
