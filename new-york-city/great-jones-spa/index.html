Great Jones Spa

Type: Spa · Neighborhood: NoHo, Manhattan

Price: from $60

DIP Score: 7.8

Hours / Access: Mon,Thu–Sun 9am–9:30pm · Wed from 2pm

A NoHo classic running the same play for twenty years, and it still works. The three-story indoor waterfall is absurdly good for a city spa. Weekday passes are some of the best value in Manhattan wellness. Unpretentious, reliable, and right there on Great Jones Street.
